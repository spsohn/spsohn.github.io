Simplicity in Nature

Things in nature I love to photograph:

  • Sunsets
  • Flowers
  • Trees
  • Bodies of Water

I started getting into photography partially through high school. Since then, I have found photography to be a hobby that allows me to relax and push aside everything going on in my life to simply focus on something else. I am fortunate enough to have a high grade DSLR camera that I can use which is how I can create breath-taking images like the ones above.
